Find Members

This menu option allows you to quickly find a member. {% if current_user.has_write_access() %} You can also update a member's details.{% endif %}

  1. Go to membership.thedonstrust.org
  2. Click on "Members"
  3. Click on "Quick find members" from the drop-down list

You will see the following screen

You can search for members by giving values in any of the column fields.
Drop-down fields "status", "type" and "country" have standard search values, the other fields are free-form text.
The "number" field is for searching by DT member number and you just need to enter the numeric part e.g. 1865
All fields apart from drop-downs can also filter by preceding the search value by one the following operators:

!=not equals
>greater than
>=greater than or equal to
<less than
<= less than or equal to

example

To find all senior members that live in SW19 who joined after 1st Jan 2010, fill in the relevant search fields and click the magnifying glass .  

If more results are found than will fit on the screen, you can use the navigation buttons on the right to flip through "pages".
