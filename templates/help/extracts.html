This menu item allows you to create csv file extracts for a variety of uses. There are two preconfigured standard categories and a custom view that allows you to control what is extracted.

For all options, the data selected for the extract is shown in preview with a button to create the extract file.

Extracts | Standard (regular)

Extracts needed on a regular basis
Email Email addresses for all members (senior and junior) who have elected to receive email comms
Comms Email and postal addresses for all members (senior and junior). Includes field to indicate email/post preference
Junior comms As above but Juniors only
Junior birthdays Extract for sending birthday cards to Juniors that have birthdays in the following month. To get a list of those with birthdays other than next month, you can add "/{m}" (where {m} is a month number) to the url shown in the browser address when selecting this option. e.g. https://membership.thedonstrust.org/extracts/junior_birthdays/5 for May birthdays.

Extracts | Standard (annual)

Extracts required annually when preparing for a new membership year
Cards (new year) Membership card details for all current members for sending to the printers
Renewal notices Mail merge file for annual renewal notices
Debits All expected direct debit payments for the new membership year for reconciliation with debit company

Extracts | Custom

This menu option allows you to create an extract of the database

  1. Click on "Extracts"
  2. Click on "Custom" from the drop-down list
